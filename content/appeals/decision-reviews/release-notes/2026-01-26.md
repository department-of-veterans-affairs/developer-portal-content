The Final Status (`finalStatus`) attribute has been added to the `/notice_of_disagreements/evidence_submission/{uuid}`, and `/supplemental_claims/evidence_submission/{uuid}` endpoints of the API. This attribute indicates whether the status of an evidence submission is final. It will default to `false` unless one of the following conditions is met:
- The evidence sbumission reaches VBMS.
- The evidence sbumission has reached the success status but will not be uploaded into VBMS.
- The evidence sbumission expired.
- The evidence sbumission has errored with a DOC1XX code.

Evidence submissions with a `finalStatus` of `true` will no longer update to a new status.
